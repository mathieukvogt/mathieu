MATHIEU

Built with frustration. Refined by obsession. Shipped with love. That's how every project starts — and ends.

I'm Mathieu, and this entire site, every line, every hover effect, every layout choice — is written by hand in vanilla JS, HTML, and CSS. Not because it's the most efficient way, but because it's the most honest way I know to build.

For me, building web apps isn't just about writing code. It's about bringing ideas to life, paying close attention to every interaction, and trying to get the little details right... even the ones most people won't notice. I believe good design tells a story, and I try to make every UI I build feel intentional — like it fits, like it belongs, even if it's not perfect.

I taught myself full-stack development over the past few years, driven by the need to turn ideas into something real. That need has turned into an obsession — with tight interfaces, subtle animations, and design that makes people feel something.

I don't see a divide between code and design. They're both part of the same craft — shaping the experience from the inside out. Whether it's a small internal tool or a public-facing app, I want the work to speak. To feel deliberate. To feel like someone cared.

Because I do.
